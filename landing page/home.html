Our services

With a mission to protect, promote, and develop the interests of Sri Lankan migrant workers, the SLBFE offers a wide range of services to facilitate and regulate the overseas employment of Sri Lankan workers while ensuring the welfare and rights of those seeking employment opportunities abroad.

Recruitment Programs of SLBFE

SLBFE is promoting ethical recruitment practices, providing essential training, and ensuring support during employment and upon return. The SLBFE strives to open up new avenues for Sri Lankan migrant workers in the overseas employment market.

Israel

Recruitment of Sri Lankan temporary workers to work in Israel

Japan

Recruitment of Sri Lankan to Japan under TITP and SSW

Korea

Sending Sri Lankan workers to South Korea under EPS program (HRD)
